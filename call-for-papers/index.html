CALL FOR SPEAKERS

TECHinPORTO is back for a new edition and we need you to make it better!

Speaking slots

  • Keynotes
  • - Opening and closing sessions each day of the conference
  • - Motivational, big ideas, inspirational, and community related topics
  • - Full conference audience
  • - 25 minutes length
  • - No Q&A
  • Talk sessions
  • - Technical, educational, research, new technology, case studies
  • - Partial conference audience (split across 2 tracks)
  • - 30 minutes length
  • - 10 minutes Q&A

Deadline

The call for speakers deadline is MARCH 31 2018

What and how

  • We are looking for tech topics:
  • - Development
  • - AI/Machine Learning
  • - IoT
  • - Security
  • - Sysadmins/Devops
  • - and so on... feel free to submit something interesting for the tech community
  • To have an eligible application, please send an e-mail to info@techinporto.com considering:
  • - Proposed title
  • - Describe what you want to present as detailed as possible
  • - Suggested main topic
  • - Target audience: who the presentation is for
  • - Speaker(s): biography, contact details and hi-res headshot
  • - The Presentation must be in English. We don't need the slides yet!
  • - MUST NOT be a sales pitch!
