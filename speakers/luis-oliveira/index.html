Luís Picciochi Oliveira

Software Engineer @ Feedzai

Bio

With a solid background in Software Engineering and Distributed Systems, Luís is one of the engineers most involved in performance testing, benchmarking and performance tuning at Feedzai.
His contributions have served to improve the reliability, repeatability and trustworthiness of performance tests at Feedzai, letting their clients rest assured that the software they run can really cope with the load that is demanded from it.

Evolving Performance Engineering processes

At Feedzai, we produce software capable of processing thousands of transactions per second at millisecond latencies.
The ways we ensure our software corresponds to this expectation are multiple and have been evolving with time.
I'll be speaking about the evolution of the methodologies we've used at Feedzai, the current different ways we do it and ways forward we foresee that we can apply to ensuring our software continues performing at the rates and speed we need it to.
