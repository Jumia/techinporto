Native vs PWA: Fight!

Liliana Sousa

We’re in a mobile era. PWA are recent and not everyone is aware of what are the advantages / disadvantages of them. My idea is to make a presentation with a peer, with a Mortal Kombat theme where we will have 6 rounds of fight: Performance, Retrocompatibility, Hardware, UI & UX, Development, Maintenance. Only in the end we will see who wins… but i promise we will have at least one fatality!
