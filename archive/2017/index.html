ABOUT TECHINPORTO

TECHINPORTO was born from Jumia's will to share and promote Porto's state of the art Information Technologies. By teaming up with great technology partners, we expect to bring the best from the tech community to our blooming city, learn together and deliver a kickass experience.

Jumia, as the biggest online services ecosystem in Africa, choose Porto to establish its tech center of excellence. For five years we achieved a lot in the African continent and in the Porto tech scene. We feel that now is the time to give back to the community.

Join us in this awesome journey and contribute to make Tech in Porto great!
